Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Turkana Community TV station is to provide a platform for the members of the community to express themselves, share their stories, and showcase their talents through various forms of media such as television programs, films, documentaries, and news broadcasts. The community TV station aims to inform, educate, entertain, and engage the local audience while promoting community involvement and participation. It serves as a platform for local organizations, businesses, and individuals to promote their events, services, and initiatives while fostering a sense of community and belonging. Additionally, community TV stations can provide valuable training opportunities for aspiring media professionals and offer a diverse range of programming that reflects the community's unique perspectives and experiences.

Audience

The audience for Turkana community TV station are the members of the local community in which the station is based. This includes residents, local businesses, organizations, and institutions such as schools, churches, and government offices. The station may also attract viewers from neighboring communities who share common interests with the local community. The audience may include individuals from diverse age groups, ethnicities, and backgrounds, who are interested in learning about local news, events, and activities, as well as discovering the talents and stories of their fellow community members. The station may also have a niche audience that is interested in specific programming such as cultural events, music, sports, or education. Overall, the audience for a community TV station is typically diverse, engaged, and interested in supporting and celebrating their local community.
